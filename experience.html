YOUR COMPLETE TRANSFORMATION PACKAGE

NUTRITION

To ensure your success, you will be working closely with a nutritionist and accountability coach. Loai, our founder is not only a nutritionist but also an experienced chef. You'll quickly learn that healthy food doesn't need to be boring, bland or 'cold chicken and broccoli'! With the choice of many delicious recipes, acquiring life long kitchen skills whilst becoming healthier for the long term. Our nutrition systems are far more educational than just a diet plan.

EVOLT 360 SCANNER

At Re-Shape we pride ourselves on having the best equipment and technology. Our Evolt360 body scanner provides all the in depth details we need to create your plan. Regular scans will hold you accountable to your goals and help your coaches make the changes necessary along the way. Your scan results will include your body fat and visceral fat measurements, muscle mass percentage, hydration, nutrient deficiency and more.

PERSONAL TRAINING

At Re-Shape, we have a team of transformation coaches who are focused on helping you transform your body, health and fitness for good. Unlike personal trainers at commercial gyms who are not necessarily trained to help you with your goals, but to just deliver sessions that may or may not be effective. Training is a skill and it is best passed on by passionate professionals who care about the small details of what will make your bespoke, results driven program a success.
